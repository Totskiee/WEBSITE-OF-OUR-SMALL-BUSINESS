Our Story

Welcome to our online home, where we celebrate two cherished Filipino delicacies: Puto Maya and Sikwate.

Puto Maya is a traditional sticky rice cake from Cebu, made with glutinous rice, coconut milk, and ginger. It's often enjoyed with slices of ripe mango. Sikwate is a rich Filipino hot chocolate crafted from pure cacao tablets known as tablea. Together, they form a beloved pairing deeply rooted in Filipino culture.
